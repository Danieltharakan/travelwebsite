Our Packages

MUNNAR

Munnar is a picturesque town located in the Western Ghats of Kerala. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, tea plantations, wildlife, and adventure activities.

ALLEPPEY

Located on the banks of the prismatic Vembanad Lake, this gorgeous city is the 'Mecca' for the backwater vacationers on a Kerala trip

WILD LIFE

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is the second largest wildlife sanctuary in Kerala. It is bestowed with lush green forests and rich wildlife.

ATHIRAPPILLY

Athirappilly Falls is the largest waterfall in Kerala and is nicknamed "The Niagara of South India".
